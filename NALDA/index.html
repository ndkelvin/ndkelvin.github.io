NALDA Volunteer Programme

The NALDA Volunteer programme provides opportunities for eligible Nigerians to contribute their volunteer actions, where suitable, to support the work of NALDA in the three main NALDA pilot projects

Buhari Young Farmers Network

Buhari Young Farmers Network intends to start this pilot program with 100 young Nigerians from each local government with a target of 2000 young Nigerians per Local Government

The Programme which will last 6 months will tactically organize broad-based volunteer services of eligible Nigerians to meet the targets of the three main pilot projects of NALDA to guarantee food and nutrition security in Nigeria.
